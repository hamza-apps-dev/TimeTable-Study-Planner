### Introduction
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This policy explains how information is handled within the TimeTable โ Study Planner app.

### Information We Collect
- We do **not** collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.
- All data (classes, schedules, calendar notes, tests, files) is stored **locally** on the user's device only.
- No data is transmitted to external servers unless explicitly initiated by the user (e.g., sharing a backup file).

### Use of Data
- Data is used **solely** to organize your study schedule.
- No data is shared with third parties for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose.

### Advertisements
- The app may display advertisements.
- Ad providers may use non-personal information to show relevant ads.
- We do not share personal data with ad networks.

### Notifications & Reminders
- The app does **not** send notifications automatically.
- Notifications are sent **only** if the user manually sets:
  - A reminder for a calendar note
  - A reminder for a class
  - A reminder for a test/exam
- Enabling or disabling reminders is **fully controlled by the user**.
- If no reminder is set, the app will **not** send any notifications.

### Backup & Restore
- When using the backup feature, data is saved as a file **on the user's device** or in a location chosen by the user.
- No data is uploaded to our servers.
- Users are responsible for securing their backup files.

### Data Security
- We take reasonable measures to protect user data within the app's local storage.
- All information remains under the **user's complete control**.
- We recommend regularly backing up your data.

- **Expo Notifications:** For local reminder notifications

- **Expo AV:** For audio/video functionality

### Children's Privacy
This app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
